# Research Brief

Create a concise brief with a clear question, evidence, analysis, uncertainty, and recommended next step. Do not invent sources or claim verification that did not occur.

## Workflow

1. State the research question, intended audience, decision, and constraints.
2. Separate supplied evidence from assumptions and missing information.
3. Read [references/evidence-rubric.md](references/evidence-rubric.md) before judging evidence quality.
4. Produce a brief using [assets/brief-template.md](assets/brief-template.md).
5. If structured headings are needed repeatedly, use `scripts/format_outline.py`; otherwise write the structure directly.

## Output rules

- Label inferences as inferences.
- List the most important uncertainty or missing evidence.
- Keep the recommendation actionable and proportionate to the evidence.
- Do not expose private reasoning traces.
